2. Design Process

Vector's interface was shaped through a minimal, high-contrast design approach focused on function and identity. Every decision, from layout to microinteractions was made to support fast actions, clean structure, and visual distinction.

Research

Identified user needs and market patterns to define functional priorities.

Ideation

Explored layout structures and visual directions through rapid sketching and flow mapping.

Visual Design

Developed a bold, abstract UI system with modular components, vibrant accents and clean spacing.

Testing
